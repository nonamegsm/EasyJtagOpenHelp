NAND Kit is a EasyJTAG Plus addon for for repairing / programming / reading / upgrading iPhone and iPad NAND / ICs Chips.
NAND Kit supports all known protocols, even unique protocols such as PPN (Perfect Page New) and NAND protocols,
especially for the LGA52/LGA60 and TSOP-48 packages. NAND Kit is Free Addon , users need just buy cheap adapter and begin work with NAND chips as soon as it arrive to thir service center

NAND Kit Software Features:
  • Smart Identify NAND Chip Type,Size and Geometry.
  • Read/Write/Erase (Full Image) for PPN , ONFI , JEDEC and Simpe NAND IC.
  • Read/Write/Extract Boot for Apple PPN NAND IC.
  • Upgrade / Replace PPN Memory (see supported models).
  • Serial Number Repair after Upgrade or Replace NANC IC.
  • Wi-Fi MAC ID Repair after Upgrade or Replace NANC IC.
  • Bluetooth MAC ID Repair after Upgrade or Replace NANC IC.
  • Region ID Repair after Upgrade or Replace NANC IC.
  • Model/Sub-Model ID Repair after Upgrade or Replace NANC IC.
  • Repair NAND Disk Errors.
  • Fix iTunes DFU and Recovery Error 9/14/20.
Supported Models:
  • iPhone 4/4S/5/5C/5S/6/6 Plus
  • iPad 2/3/4/5/6
  • iPad Mini 1/2/3/4
  • iPad Air/iPad Air2

We did not support PCI-e based devices:
iPhone 6s,7,7 Plus,8,8 Plus and iPhone X.
