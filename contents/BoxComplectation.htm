EasyJTAG Plus box complectation

 

What included with EasyJTAG Plus box

  • Easy JTAG Plus Box (Metal Edition).
  • JTAG Soldering connector.
  • ISP Soldering connector 1bit.
  • ISP Soldering connector 4bit (4bit mode is not released on SW now)
  • 20P to 20P "E" type Adapter (for connect third-party sockets or PCB).
  • 20P to 20P "U" type Adapter (for connect UFI box pinout sockets or PCB for soldering).
  • Free access to our file support servers.
  • Z3X Certified universal eMMC 6in1 socket (not included at "Lite" box).
  • Z3X Smart Card with EasyJTAG Activation (not included at "Upgrade" set).
