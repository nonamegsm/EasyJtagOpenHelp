JTAG Pinout Helper

JTAG Pinout tab - Shows a picture with marked pads for soldering the Data wires and GND ("Ground")  wire.

TMS, TCK, TDI, TDO, TRST, SRST   are the Data lines.

Important! The GND wire and the Data wires should be as short as possible.

Also the GND wire must be thicker than Data wires.

The picture shows a typical JTAG Pinout
