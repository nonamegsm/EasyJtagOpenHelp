NANDkit adapter (Only for EasyJTAG Plus box)

NANDkit adapter is native Z3x adapter for work with NAND chips at TSOP48 and LGA52 cases. NANDkit adapter is produced by Z3x team without LGA52 socket.

 

 

  1. Main NAND adapter PCB with standarted connector
  2. TSOP48-0.5 Interposer Board Adapter
  3. Socket for TSOP48 x8 NAND
  4. 2x PCB for TSOP48 and LGA52 chips soldering
  5. LGA52 socket - is third-party socket for NANDkit adapter
  6. Pins for PCB and mountings for main NAND adapter board
