Update eMMC Firmware

EasyJTAG Classic software can make Update Firmware for new Samsung eMMC with ffu (Flash Firmware Update) file.

FFU firmware update procedure not wipe data and not wipe RPMB area.

 

List of EasyJTAG firmware update supported eMMC can see at EasyJTAG Classic software directory and "eMMC Firmware" subfolder.

 

NOTE: eMMC firmware update does not restore the resource of the chip cells, which have a limited number of write-erase cycles.
Thus, if the cells of the chip have exhausted their resource, or are close to the limit, then the life of such a chip is limited to weeks or months.
