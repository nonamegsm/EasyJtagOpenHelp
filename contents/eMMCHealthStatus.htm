eMMC Health Status

eMMC Flash memory based on NAND technology and have limited cycles of erase. Number of cycles is depend by cell tipe (SLC/MLC/TLC/QLC) and tech-process resolutions (130-90-54-36nm and lover). 

 

Number of erase cycles for different memory types:

Type Cycles
SLC NAND 100 000
MLC NAND 1000 .. 10 000
TLC NAND 1000
QLC NAND 100 .. 1000
3D MLC NAND 6000 .. 40 000
3D TLC NAND

1000 .. 3000

 

At the end of lifetime the cells go to Read-Only state and can't be normally use.

 

Types of Health status reports:

  • All new eMMC 5.0, 5.01 and 5.1 revision have standardized  Health Status report.
  • Samsung eMMC 4.1-4.51 revision and some Samsung eMMC 5.0 have internal Samsung Health Status report
  • Sandisk eMMC 4.51 and some lover revision have Health Status report
  • Other brand eMMC 4.51 and lover revision have no Health Status report

Typically eMMC 5.0-5.1 and Sandisk up to 4.51 revision Health Status report:

====================

Device Life Time Estimation (MLC) [269]: 0x01 0% - 10% device life time used
Device Life Time Estimation (SLC) [268]: 0x01 0% - 10% device life time used
Pre EOL information [267]: 0x01 Normal

Pre EOL information: overall status for reserved blocks. Possible values are:

  • 0x00 - Not defined.
  • 0x01 - Normal: consumed less than 80% of the reserved blocks.
  • 0x02 - Warning: consumed 80% of the reserved blocks.
  • 0x03 - Urgent: consumed 90% of the reserved blocks

====================

 

eMMC can have SLC (Sinlgle Level Cells) cells or/and MLC cells (MLC (Multi Level Cells) cells is MCL/TLC/QLC), and eMMC is reporting about lifetyme of those types of cells. Lifetyme of cells can't be restore. 

Note: Samsung eMMC after firmware reflash procedure is reset lifetime counter, but it not repair eMMC cells, only reset counter.

Describe this report: This chip have 1 array of cells (Total Number of banks: 0x1), have 3 bad block from factory at 1st array (Initial bad blocks per bank: 3,0,0,0), have 0 new bad block (Runtime bad blocks per bank: 0,0,0,0 ) and 23 free blocks space for remap bad blocks (Reserved blocks left per bank : 23,0,0,0). This chip have  43416  average erase cycles of erase (for SLC max 50 000- 100 000) and 432 average erase cycles for MLC cells (of 1000-10 000 max).

 

Result: This chip have 40-80% cells lifetime use and Normal Pre EOL status.
