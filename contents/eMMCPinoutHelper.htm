eMMC Pinout Helper

eMMC Pinout tab - Shows a picture with marked pads for soldering the Data wires and Power wires.

 

2.8V, 1.8V,  GND ("Ground") are the Power lines;

 

CLK, DAT0, CMD are the Data lines.

 

Important! The Power wires and the Data wires should be as short as possible.

 

Also the Power wires must be thicker than Data wires.
