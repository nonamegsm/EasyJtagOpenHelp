eMMC Socket

EasyJTAG Plus box have native 6in1 socket (include at "Full" version). 

  1. Socket head
  2. EasyJTAG Plus adapter for socket head
  3. Mountings and alignment underlay for adapter

 

This socket support eMMC/eMCP at next BGA cases:

  • BGA153 case 10x10mm, 11,5x13mm 
  • BGA169 case 12x16mm, 12x18mm
  • BGA162 case 11,5x13mm 
  • BGA186 case 12x16mm
  • BGA221 case 11,5x13mm 
  • BGA529 case 15x15mm
